Bacon Lettuce Tomato Sandwich (BLT)

Go Back

Description

The BLT is an American favorite served at any casual diner. The name might be simple but it is a delicious meal to have for picnics or any other lunch settings.

Ingredients

Makes 2 Sandwiches

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 2-3 leaves of lettuce
  • 1 ripe tomato
  • 1-2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 slices of bread

Steps

  1. The Bacon
    1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add the bacon slices and cook until crispy, about 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally.
    3. Transfer the cooked bacon to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess grease.
  2. The Lettuce and Tomato
    1. Wash and dry the lettuce leaves.
    2. Slice the tomato into even rounds.
  3. Assembly
    1. Spread mayonnaise evenly on one side of each slice of bread.
    2. On one slice of bread, layer the lettuce leaves.
    3. Add the tomato slices on top of the lettuce. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
    4. Place the crispy bacon slices on top of the tomato.
    5. Top with the second slice of bread, mayonnaise side down.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, consider adding sliced avocado or a slice of your favorite cheese.
  • To make it healthier, use whole grain bread and low-fat mayonnaise.
